MOM ARE YOU THERE?

Three daughters of mother Gaia are standing next to the bed of their mom, contemplating how to wake her up out of her oblivion. All bringing the meeting to different places and times, they collide at this crucial moment, attempting to bring their mother out of her vegetable state, by proposing different plans and beliefs to ameliorate the situation on earth.
